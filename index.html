Privacy Policy

How we protect and respect your personal information and devotional data.

Last updated: April 1, 2025. Your privacy is sacred to us. This policy explains clearly what we collect, how we use it, and your rights over your data.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account or use Sanatan, you may provide us with information such as your name, email address, phone number, date of birth (for personalised horoscope), and preferred language. This information helps us personalise your spiritual experience.

Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App, including your device type, operating system, app version, session duration, features used, and approximate location (city-level, for festival and temple recommendations). We do not track your precise GPS location without explicit permission.

Spiritual Preferences

The mantras you save, deities you follow, and content you listen to — these preferences are stored solely to personalise your experience and are never shared with advertisers or third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Sanatan App and its features
  • To personalise content, recommendations, and notifications
  • To send relevant alerts such as festival reminders and muhurat notifications
  • To provide customer support and respond to your queries
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve app performance
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your spiritual preferences for advertising profiling of any kind.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit). We regularly audit our security practices and maintain SOC 2 compliance. Offline content you download is stored locally on your device.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will erase your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our app (such as cloud hosting, analytics, and payment processing). All third-party providers are bound by strict data processing agreements. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time through the app's Settings section. You may also opt out of marketing communications, withdraw consent for data processing, and request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sanatanapp.in.

6. Children's Privacy

The Sanatan App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about our privacy practices? Email us at privacy@sanatanapp.in or write to: Sanatan Technologies Pvt. Ltd., 4th Floor, Ganesh Plaza, Navrangpura, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380009.

Our Story

About Sanatan

Born from devotion, built with technology — bridging eternal wisdom and the digital age.

Our Mission

Sanatan was born from a simple, heartfelt belief: that the ancient wisdom of our scriptures and the divine vibrations of our mantras should be accessible to every devotee — wherever they are, in whatever language they speak.

Founded in Ahmedabad in 2020 by a team of technology professionals and spiritual practitioners, we set out to create the most comprehensive, authentic, and beautifully designed spiritual companion for the modern Hindu family.

"सनातन धर्म का सार — ज्ञान, भक्ति, और कर्म। हम इसे आपके जीवन का हिस्सा बनाना चाहते हैं।"

Our Journey

2020 — Sanatan App launched on Android with 1,000 mantras and basic Panchang. Reached 50,000 downloads in the first month.

2021 — Expanded to iOS. Added bhajan library with 5,000+ tracks. Introduced Rashifal and daily horoscope features.

2022 — Crossed 500,000 users. Added support for 10 Indian languages. Launched offline download feature.

2023 — Partnered with 200+ temples across India for live darshan streaming. Reached 1 million active users.

2024 — Crossed 2 million downloads. Launched premium subscription with ad-free experience and exclusive content from revered artists.

2025 — Continuing to grow with new features, new languages, and deeper integration of Vedic astrology and sacred texts.
